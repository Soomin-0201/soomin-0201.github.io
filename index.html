ABOUT

Computer Science graduate student at Georgia Tech, focused on parallel computing and HPC systems.

My research centers on the thermal modeling of cryogenic fluid systems — building modular Python simulations that couple convection, radiation, and conduction with CFD-derived surrogate models.

Outside research, I'm deep in parallel algorithms — MPI collectives, hypercube routing, sample sort — and the engineering craft of making scientific code clean, modular, and fast.
